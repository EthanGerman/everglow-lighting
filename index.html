FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you want to know before booking your free estimate. Still have questions? Just call us.

Call Us Directly
How long does installation take?▾
Most homes are done in 2–4 hours. Larger properties may take a full day. We'll give you an accurate timeline at your free consultation.
What does it cost?▾
Pricing is based on linear footage of your roofline. Most homes run $1,500–$3,500. We offer financing. Your free estimate gives you an exact number — no pressure, no surprise fees.
Do the lights work in all weather?▾
Yes. InvisiLights are IP67-rated waterproof, UV-resistant, and rated for extreme temperatures. Built for permanent outdoor installation in any climate.
What's the warranty?▾
InvisiLights carry a 25-year product warranty. Our installation is also guaranteed. If anything fails due to a product defect, it's covered — full stop.
Can I schedule them automatically?▾
Absolutely. The InvisiLights app lets you schedule lights to turn on at sunset, off at a set time, change colors for holidays automatically — completely hands-free.
